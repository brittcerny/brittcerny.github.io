Our Map

This interactive map provides the spatial framework for the Urban Cookie Forest project in Phoenix, Arizona. At this stage of development, the map displays the boundary of the City of Phoenix to establish the geographic extent of the project area.

As the project progresses, this map will be updated with point locations of newly planted trees collected through field data surveys. These data will help track planting activity, visualize the growth of the Urban Cookie Forest, and support future urban forestry efforts across the city.
