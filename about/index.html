Our Goal

Our goal is to plant a tree for every Girl Scout group in partnership with a Better Business Bureau–accredited business, creating meaningful connections between young women and their local communities. Beyond building relationships, this initiative directly contributes to improving urban environments by increasing tree canopy, reducing heat, and supporting healthier ecosystems. By bringing together leadership, service, and responsible business practices, each tree becomes both a symbol of collaboration and a step toward a more sustainable future. Together, these efforts will grow into the Urban Cookie Forest.

Why It Matters

Trees play a vital role in improving urban environments in both visible and invisible ways. They provide shade that cools streets and buildings, helping reduce urban heat and lower energy use. Their leaves and roots intercept stormwater, reducing runoff and helping prevent flooding while improving water quality. Trees also enhance air quality, support local wildlife, and add natural beauty to neighborhoods, making communities healthier, cooler, and more enjoyable places to live.
